MaFS origins in the US

Married at First Sight first premiered on July 8, 2014.

It has since had 16 seasons.

The program featured three couples for the first seven seasons, MAFS has had four or more couples since season eight.

The show follows six or more people who simultaneously meet and walk down the aisle.

By the end of each season, the cast members are tasked with deciding to stay together or divorce.

Clint is ‘disappointed’

After Gina confessed to Clint that she didn’t feel physically attracted to him, the new husband admitted in a confessional that he’s “disappointed” in the news.

For her to verbalize that so early in a relationship, I’m disappointed,” he said.

“But we’re married, so I mean, we’re committed to this and we’re gonna do whatever it takes to try and make it work.”

Gina reveals she’s not attracted to Clint

In a recent episode, Gina admitted to Clint that she doesn’t feel “overwhelming physical chemistry” with him.

The bride said: “At the end of the day, I don’t know how you’re feeling, but I’m not really feeling this overwhelming physical chemistry, for sure. I don’t wanna force anything, so I don’t know. How do you feel on that?”

Clint responded that he “feels the same way,” saying: “I mean, the experts matched us up for a reason, so we’ll see what happens. We might go through something all of the sudden that changes our perspective.”

Nicole and Chris discuss anger issues

As the conversation continued, Nicole asked Chris if it bothered him that she could “lose her cool” easily.

The MAFS star answered: “I don’t know. I mean, I honestly don’t know. But if you’re taking it out on me, I’m not going to stand for it. I’m going to tell you right now I’m not going to take it.”

She replied: “You shouldn’t, good.”

In a confessional, Chris said: “I feel like Old Nicole would have been a problem for me, to be honest. I mean, the selfishness, and her being a little hot-headed, and a little intense – It’s definitely something in the back of my mind to keep an eye out for.”

Chris concluded: “Because it’s something I’ve dealt with before and something I definitely do not want.”

Back addressing Nicole, Chris said: “I don’t want to be in a situation where you let your emotions get the best of you.”

Nicole’s shocking confession

During a recent episode, Chris wondered if “Old Nicole” would be a problem in their marriage.

Nicole opened the conversation by discussing her troubled past during the couple’s rum tasting on their honeymoon.

She began: “This brings me back to a time that I call Old Nicole.”

Chris asked: “What’s Old Nicole?”

His new wife explained: “Old Nicole was drinking a lot and I was pretty selfish because I wanted everything to be about me enjoying my best life.

“I was out at bars all of the time and just drinking and really living it up and once I hit 28 that’s when I started my whole personal growth journey.”

Get to know Christopher and Nicole

Nicole, 32, and Christopher, 36, are a couple on MAFS season 16.

Nicole is a New York City transplant who had enough of the large cities “toxic” dating scene.

She is a senior marketing advisor who the show’s website describes as “ready to focus on dating again and find true love.”

Christopher is a business coach that also relocated from Chicago, Illinois.

The website says that he has had a hard time finding love because of the Covid pandemic.
