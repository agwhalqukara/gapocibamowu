American actress, Holland Roden came into the limelight after starring as Lydia Martin in the famous MTV's teen drama series, Teen Wolf from Season 1 to Season 6. More to that, she is known for her guest role Bronwyn in 12 Miles of Bad Road in 2008 and as Bridget Cleary in Amazon Prime Video's horror series Lore in 2017.

Is Holland Roden Dating? Her Past Relationships

34 years old Holland Rode is not a married woman. For now, she is single who is enjoying her singledom to the fullest. Likewise, she has one pet dog named Fievel, with whom she shares trip photos on her Instagram account. She has 5.4m Instagram followers as of now.

Nonetheless, Roden had two relationships in the past that we are aware of. In February 2013, she started dating her Teen Wolf's co-star, Ian Bohen, 44, after they met on the set of Teen Wolf. However, they spilt in 2012 after nearly dating for two years.

Two years later, the 1.6 m tall actress again found love in the arms of her former Teen Wolf co-star Max Carver on May 15, 2014. But they also ended their relationship in 2016 after a togetherness of nearly two years. 

Following her two break-ups, the star Holland is quite busy with her career.

Holland Roden: Bio and Early Life

Holland Roden was born on the 7th of October 1986, in Dallas, Texas, the United States to American parents as Holland Marie Roden. She has three siblings, one sister Taylor Roden and two brothers Scott Roden and Ryan Ryan Roden.

Roden attended the all-girls private school Hockaday School in Dallas, Texas. Then she graduated from the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA) with a major in molecular biology and women's studies. 

So before starting her acting career, she studied pre-medical education to be a cardiothoracic surgeon for three and a half years.
