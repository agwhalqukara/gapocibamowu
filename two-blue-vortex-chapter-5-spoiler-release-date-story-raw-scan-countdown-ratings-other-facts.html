Recap of Previous Chapter 4

Boruto: Two Blue Vortex Chapter 4 was titled “The Divine Tree”. The chapter showed:

  • Boruto infiltrating the Claw Grimes base, where he found a giant cage that contained the Ten-Tails, a monstrous beast that can create a divine tree that produces a godlike fruit.
  • Boruto being attacked by four clones of Claw Grimes members, who were created by the chakra of the people who were bitten by the Grimes. The clones had access to the original members’ powers and personalities, and they introduced themselves as Koji, Sumire, Mitsuki, and Shikadai.
  • Boruto fighting the clones, but he was overpowered by their abilities and teamwork. He was saved by the arrival of Sarada, who had followed him to the base. Sarada used her Sharingan to analyze the clones’ weaknesses and helped Boruto defeat them.
  • Boruto and Sarada reaching the cage, where they saw the Ten-Tails. Boruto recognized the beast as the same one that he and Naruto fought in the past, and he wondered why Claw Grimes had it. He decided to free the Ten-Tails, hoping that it would lead him to Sasuke.
  • Boruto using his Karma seal to open the cage, but he was shocked to see that the Ten-Tails had evolved into a new form that resembled a tree with a mouth. The Ten-Tails sensed Boruto’s presence and attacked him with its tentacles.
  • Boruto being saved by Sasuke, who appeared behind him and cut off the tentacles. Sasuke revealed that he had been investigating Claw Grimes and their plan to use the Ten-Tails to create a new world order. He told Boruto that they had to stop them before it was too late.
