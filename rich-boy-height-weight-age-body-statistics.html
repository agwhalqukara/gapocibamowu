Rich Boy is an American rapper who shot to fame with the release of his single, Throw Some D’s, which peaked in various music charts like at #3 in the U.S. Hot R&B /Hip-Hop Songs (Billboard), #2 in U.S. Hot Rap Songs (Billboard), and #6 in U.S. Billboard Hot 100. He has also released various other singles, mixtapes, and studio albums including Break the Pot, Bigger Than the Mayor, Pacc Man, Bring It To The Block, 12 Diamonds, Gold Kilo$, Boy Looka Here, Good Things, and Drop. Rich boy has more than 60k subscribers on YouTube and more than 50k followers on Instagram.

First Album

He released his self-titled debut album, Rich Boy, on March 13, 2007, via Zone 4 and Interscope Records. The album reached #3 on the U.S. Billboard 200 list, #3 on U.S. Top R&B /Hip-Hop Albums (Billboard) list, and #1 on U.S. Rap Albums (Billboard) list. It had also sold 332,000 copies by the week of June 17, 2007.

First TV Show

He made his first TV show appearance as himself in the music series, 106 & Park Top 10 Live, in 2006.
