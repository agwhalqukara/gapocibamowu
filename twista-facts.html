Singer’s Bio

Twista, born as Carl Mitchell in Chicago, Illinois, is a legendary rapper best known for his electrifying rapid-fire flow. He was born on November 27, 1973, which makes him currently 47 years old. Twista started rapping at a young age and was discovered by producer Toxic of the songwriting and production team The Legendary Traxster.

Age, Relationships & Children

Many fans are curious about Twista’s age and personal life. The rapper has always been tight-lipped about his relationships, but it is known that he has a daughter named Ashley Mitchell. Twista is not married and has kept his romantic life private.

Height

Twista’s height is another topic that fans are curious about. The rapper is 5 feet 8 inches tall, which is considered average for a man.

Career

Twista’s career spans over three decades, and he has released ten studio albums to date. In the early years, he gained popularity as part of the hip-hop group “Do or Die” with the hit single “Po Pimp.” However, his solo career took off in the early 2000s with his album “Kamikaze,” which sold over two million copies worldwide. Twista’s unique style of rapping made him stand out in the crowd and paved the way for other fast rappers like Tech N9ne.

Top Songs

Over the years, Twista has created many hits that continue to be popular today. Some of his most famous tracks include “Slow Jamz” featuring Kanye West and Jamie Foxx, “Overnight Celebrity,” “Adrenaline Rush,” “Hope,” and “Wetter” featuring Erika Shevon.

Twista’s music has influenced many other rappers, including Kendrick Lamar, Jay-Z, and Nas, who have all mentioned him as an inspiration.

Net Worth

Twista’s net worth is estimated to be around $7 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth. He has earned this wealth through his successful music career and various entrepreneurial pursuits.

Philanthropy

Twista is not only a talented rapper but also a compassionate individual who has given back to his community. In 2005, he started the Twista Foundation, which aims to help at-risk youth through education, mentorship, and various programs. The rapper has also donated money to help victims of Hurricane Katrina and has worked with other charities, such as 100 Suits for 100 Men and St. Jude Children’s Research Hospital.

Influences

Like any other artist, Twista also draws inspiration from other musicians. In an interview with XXL Magazine, he said that he had learned a lot from Michael Jackson, Prince, Rakim, and Kool G Rap, who had all played a role in shaping his musical style. He has also mentioned that growing up in Chicago helped him develop his sound, and he often raps about the violence and poverty that he witnessed in his city.

The Fastest Rapper Alive

One of the things that Twista is most famous for is his lightning-fast rapping speed. In 1992, he was listed in the Guinness Book of World Records as the fastest rapper in the world, with a speed of 598 syllables in 55 seconds. However, this record was broken several times over the years, and the title currently belongs to a rapper named Chopper.

Even though Twista may not have the title of the fastest rapper anymore, he is still widely recognized for his unique style and talent. His contributions to the rap genre have made him a legend and an inspiration to many aspiring rappers.

Conclusion

In conclusion, Twista’s career in the music industry has been nothing short of impressive. His rapid rapping skills, combined with his captivating storytelling and creative wordplay, have made him a household name in the hip-hop community. Twista’s music has had a profound impact on the rap genre, and he will always be remembered for his unique style and contributions to the industry.
