Dimitri Diatchenko was an American actor and musician.

Dimitri was the son of Georgette and Nikolai/Nicholas Diatchenko. His father is Ukrainian. His mother is of half Greek and half Swedish descent.

Dimitri’s maternal grandfather was named George/Geo C. Trouvalis. George was born in Greece.

Dimitri’s maternal grandmother was Elvie/Elsie G. Peterson (the daughter of David A. O. Peterson and Matilda C. Adler). Elvie was born in Portland, Maine. David was born in either Maine or Sweden, to parents from Sweden. Matilda was also born in either Maine or Sweden, to parents from Sweden.
