Who was Lisa Sheridan?

She was an American actress known for her roles in TV series such as FreakyLinks, Invasion, and Halt and Catch Fire. She also appeared in numerous guest roles on shows like CSI: Miami, The Mentalist, and Scandal.

She was born on December 5, 1974, in Macon, Georgia.

What is Lisa’s educational background?

She attended the Carnegie Mellon School of Drama in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, where she graduated with honors and received the Thomas Auclair Memorial Scholarship Award for the most promising student actor.

What are some notable roles Lisa played?

She is best known for her roles as Chloe Tanner on FreakyLinks, Larkin Groves on Invasion, and Kathleen Newberry on CSI: Miami. She also starred in the 2018 independent film Strange Nature.

What was the cause of Lisa Sheridan’s death?

When did Lisa Sheridan pass away?

She passed away on February 25, 2019, at the age of 44.
